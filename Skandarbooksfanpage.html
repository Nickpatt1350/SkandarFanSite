Skandar Series

If you love magical creatures, fierce battles, epic adventures, and friendships you’re in the right place! This site is dedicated to the incredible Skandar series by A.F. Steadman — a world where unicorns are not gentle, but wild and dangerous, bonded to young riders through elemental magic: Fire, Water, Earth, Air and Spirit. Follow the journey of Skandar Smith and his quartet Bobby, Mitchell, Flo. As uncover hidden powers, train with bonded unicorns, and face dark forces that threaten to tear the magical Island that they love apart. Whether you’re just discovering the series or have devoured every book, this is your place to dive deeper, celebrate the story, and connect with other fans of this unforgettable fantasy world.
